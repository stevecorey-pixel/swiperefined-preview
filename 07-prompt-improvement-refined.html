Make your prompts easier to respond to

Generic answers close conversations before they start. A little specificity opens them wide.

"A perfect Sunday…"
Current
Relaxing and watching Netflix.
Issue
Too generic; hard to start a conversation.
Improved
Slow coffee, a walk somewhere green, one errand I pretended I'd do yesterday, and ending with takeout from a place I'll insist is underrated.
Why it works
It gives someone multiple easy ways to reply.
"I geek out on…"
Current
Music.
Issue
Too broad to be a conversation starter — nearly everyone likes music.
Improved
Finding the one song on an album everyone skipped but should not have.
Why it works
Specific, playful, and invites a music conversation.
